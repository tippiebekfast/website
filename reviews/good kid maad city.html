good kid m.A.A.d city - Kendrick Lamar

The cover isn't kidding. This is a short film. The album follows Freytag's Pyramid and everything. It even starts and ends with the VHS being put in and taken out.

The production is amazing, it really sounds like a movie soundtrack. Emotional highs and lows - Backseat Freestyle and Sing About Me - this album makes you feel it all.

Kendrick proved his skill after Section.80. Here he's using that skill to create one of the greatest albums of all time.
I don't mean rap albums either.

10/10
1. m.A.A.d city
2. Sing About Me, I'm Dying Of Thirst
3. Bitch, Don't Kill My Vibe
