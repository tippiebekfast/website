Madvillainy - Madvillain (MF DOOM and Madlib)

I have listened to this album so many times. So much, that when my friend was listening to the album, I could tell what song and what part of that song he was up to. But it never wears off on me.

From the chilling opening of The Illest Villains, to the cinematic experience of America's Most Blunted, and the encore of Rhinestone Cowboy. This album has no skips.

That's the thing with this album - atmosphere. It immerses you in the music, it makes you feel the terror of Madvillain.

10/10
